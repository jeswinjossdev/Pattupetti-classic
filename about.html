🎵 About Pattupetti

Pattupetti is a modern, feature-rich music streaming application that brings you unlimited access to millions of songs from YouTube. Built with cutting-edge web technologies, it offers a seamless and intuitive music discovery experience across all your devices.

Whether you're looking for the latest hits, timeless classics, or discovering new artists, Pattupetti provides a beautiful, responsive interface that adapts perfectly to smartphones, tablets, laptops, and smart TVs.

Our mission is to make music accessible to everyone while providing the best possible user experience with modern design aesthetics and smooth performance.

✨ Key Features

  • 🔍
    Smart Search: Instantly find songs, artists, and albums with our intelligent search system
  • 📱
    Responsive Design: Optimized for all devices - mobile, tablet, desktop, and TV
  • 🎛️
    Compact Player: Sleek bottom-right player that expands for full controls
  • 📋
    Custom Playlists: Create and manage your personal music collections
  • ❤️
    Favorites System: Like and save your favorite tracks for easy access
  • 🔥
    Trending Music: Discover what's hot with our trending music section
  • 🔀
    Shuffle & Repeat: Customize your listening experience with playback modes
  • 🌙
    Dark Theme: Beautiful dark interface that's easy on the eyes
  • 🐛
    Bug Reporting: Easy-to-use bug report system with file upload support
  • ⌨️
    Keyboard Shortcuts: Control playback with convenient keyboard shortcuts
